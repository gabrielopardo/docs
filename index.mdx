Arc Manager helps you track materials, hardware, and components, build products with real cost breakdowns, and keep your data safe on your own computer — no internet required.

## Key Features

- **Inventory Tracking** — Manage materials, hardware, components, and finished products with real-time stock levels
- **Cost Calculation** — Automatically calculate product costs based on materials and labor, with profit margin insights
- **Bill of Materials** — Build components and products from your inventory items, with automatic cost roll-up
- **Purchase Logging** — Record purchases and track spending by month
- **Dashboard Overview** — See inventory value, out-of-stock items, top products by profit, and recent builds at a glance
- **Automatic Backups** — Your data is backed up every time you open the app, with the last 7 backups retained
- **Light & Dark Mode** — Choose the appearance that suits your workspace
